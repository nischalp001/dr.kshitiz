About Me

I'm Dr. Kshitiz Puri, a board-certified cardiologist with over 15 years of experience in clinical practice, research, and medical education. I specialize in preventive cardiology, cardiac diagnostics, and comprehensive heart health management.

After completing my medical degree at Johns Hopkins School of Medicine, I pursued specialized training through a cardiology fellowship at Massachusetts General Hospital and further advanced my expertise through research at the Stanford Cardiovascular Institute.

My approach to patient care blends evidence-based medicine with compassionate, personalized treatment plans. I believe in empowering patients through education and shared decision-making to achieve optimal heart health outcomes.
